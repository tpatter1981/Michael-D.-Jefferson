Personal Injury

When you’re injured or suffer a loss, your world is turned inside out in an instant. If your injury or loss is through the negligence of another, you face the process of a possible lawsuit and then the real work begins. While the memories are still fresh and evidence is still available, everything must be gathered to make your case and that can be difficult when you’re dealing with pain and grief at the same time.

Workers' Compensation

If you’ve been injured on the job, what are your rights? How will you know if you need an attorney? Since 1974, we’ve been protecting the rights of injured workers, and we’re here to help you navigate what can be a complex and confusing time so you get the care and compensation you need and deserve. We understand the laws of Georgia and the Southeast, so you can trust our expertise.

Wrongful Death

When someone we love is taken from us unexpectedly, the last thing we want to deal with is all of the details that come with the loss. When your loved one’s death is caused by another’s negligence or wrongdoing, the grieving becomes even more intense. There is nothing that can take the place of your loved one or make the grieving process easier, but if you are faced with this situation, you may have the grounds for a wrongful death lawsuit.

Estate Planning

When you’re planning for future generations, you need attorneys who will work closely with you, taking the time to understand you, your family, and your goals for your estate. At Deming Law, we take an individualized approach, putting you first, so that we successfully preserve and protect your assets, whether you are transferring wealth from one generation to the next, taking advantage of the latest changes in tax code, or managing charitable giving.

Who We Are

Services to Business Clients include general corporate matters, interstate commerce, mergers, tax and bankruptcy. Services to Individual Clients include wrongful death, personal injury, worker's compensation, bankruptcy, social security, wills and trusts, domestic relations and real estate.
